About Me

I am a Data Analyst with a Bachelor’s degree in Business Analysis from the University of Central Punjab (UCP), Pakistan. I specialize in transforming raw and complex datasets into meaningful, actionable business insights that support strategic decision-making. My expertise lies in data-driven analytics, dashboard development, and cloud-based data solutions using tools such as SQL, Power BI, AWS, Snowflake, and Python. I focus on building interactive dashboards and automated reporting systems that help organizations track performance, identify trends, and improve operational efficiency. I have hands-on experience in designing data models, optimizing queries, and developing ETL pipelines for scalable analytics systems. My goal is to bridge the gap between data and business by delivering clear, accurate, and visually compelling insights that drive profitability, growth, and smarter decision-making.
